Okro Soup

Description

Okra or Okro is a word and soup that is believed to have originated from the Igbo speaking people of Nigeria, since the word itself is derived from the original Igbo term — "Ọkwụrụ" or Ọ́kụ̀rụ̀

It is prepared using the edible green seed pods of the okra flowering plant as a primary ingredient. Other vegetables can be added to the soup as well, such as ewedu, kerenkere, or Ugu leaf. Depending on the specific variant being prepared, okra soup can have a clear broth or be deep green in colour, much like the okra plant itself

Ingredients

  • okro
  • palmoil
  • pepper
  • salt
  • ogiri
  • meat
  • fish
  • water

Steps

  1. wash the fresh okro
  2. slice int cubes
  3. boil small qauntity of water
  4. boil meat and fish
  5. add seasoning
  6. add palmoil
  7. add sliced okra
  8. add salt
  9. allow to simmer for 5 minutes
